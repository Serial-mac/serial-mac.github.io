How It Works

Step 1: Connect Your Device

Plug in your USB-to-serial adapter or serial hardware. Serial auto-detects it—no setup required.

Step 2: Select or Create Profile

Choose a preset or build a custom profile with baud rate, data bits, parity, and stop bits.

Step 3: Connect and Interact

Open the session. Send commands, run terminal apps, or configure devices with full emulation.

Step 4: Save and Reuse

Store macros and settings for one-click reconnection next time.
